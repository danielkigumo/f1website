2026 Season News

The biggest stories from the 2026 Formula 1 season.

Latest Stories

February 2026 — Driver Transfers

Hamilton Makes the Move to Ferrari

The biggest transfer in years became reality in 2026. Lewis Hamilton joined Scuderia Ferrari after 11 seasons with Mercedes, saying it had been a lifelong dream. Ferrari paired him with Charles Leclerc, giving them arguably their strongest driver lineup in decades. The vacant Mercedes seat went to 18-year-old Italian rookie Andrea Kimi Antonelli.

January 2026 — Pre-Season

Sainz Signs Long-Term Deal with Williams

After losing his Ferrari seat to Hamilton, Carlos Sainz chose Williams Racing over other options, signing a multi-year deal. He said he believed in the team's long-term project. Williams also retained Alexander Albon, giving them one of the more experienced midfield pairings on the 2026 grid.

March 2026 — Regulation Changes

New 2026 Rules Shake Up the Grid

The 2026 season brought sweeping technical regulation changes — smaller cars, revised aerodynamics, and a new hybrid power unit formula. Teams had spent over two years preparing. McLaren came out strongest in pre-season testing, while Red Bull showed strong race pace from round one despite the new rules catching some teams off guard.

April 2026 — Championship Update

Verstappen Leads After Eight Rounds

Max Verstappen holds the championship lead with 219 points, but Lando Norris in second is only 23 points behind — the tightest battle in years. In the constructors standings, McLaren leads Ferrari by just 26 points. With 16 races still to go, the 2026 title fight is wide open.
